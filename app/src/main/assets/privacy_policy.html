Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Asthma Manager App ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and associated services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, address
  • Health Information: Age, height, weight, asthma diagnosis details, medical history
  • PEFR Data: Peak Expiratory Flow Rate measurements and readings history
  • Contact Information: Emergency contact details
  • Baseline Measurements: Baseline PEFR values and other vital measurements

1.2 Device Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • App version and usage data

1.3 Usage Information

We collect information about how you interact with the app, including:

  • Features accessed
  • Time and duration of use
  • Actions taken within the app
  • Error logs and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve the app functionality
  • Medical Care: To help manage your asthma condition and facilitate communication with healthcare providers
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and provide relevant recommendations
  • Communication: To send you notifications, reminders, and important updates
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve our services
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, security incidents, and abuse

3. Data Security

Data Protection: Your health information is sensitive. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and user permission management
  • Secure backend infrastructure
  • Compliance with healthcare data protection standards

Note: While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Linked Healthcare Providers

If you link your account with a healthcare provider (doctor), your health information may be shared with them to facilitate your medical care.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app and providing services, such as:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics services
  • Communication services (email, SMS)
  • Payment processors

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

4.4 Business Transfers

If our company is involved in a merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Right to access your personal information
  • Correction: Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Right to request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Right to obtain your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Right to opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw Consent: Right to withdraw previously given consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section.

6. Retention of Data

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Specific retention periods include:

  • Account Information: Retained for the duration of your account and up to 2 years after deletion
  • Health Records: Retained according to applicable healthcare regulations (typically 6-7 years)
  • Usage Data: Typically retained for 12-24 months for analytics purposes

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

The Asthma Manager App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

For users under 18, parental consent is required. Parents/guardians can manage the account and access their child's health information.

You can control cookie settings through your device and app settings.

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
